# BuyerLab AI Demo Script

Use this 60-second script for hackathon judging.

Before recording, set `BUYERLAB_MOCK_MODE=true` for a deterministic demo if a Gemini API key is not available.

## Recommended Primary Demo

Use **Wireless Earbuds Demo**.

Why this sample works well:

- Brand/model-level audit: SoundPeak AirBass X2
- Heuristic local TRY price perception at 799 TRY
- Competitor gap against TuneGo Lite Pro at 699 TRY
- Missing battery life, warranty, microphone proof, and trust signals
- Clear buyer objections, launch readiness risk, and before/after improvement

## 0-10 sec: Problem

"E-commerce sellers often discover product-page problems after they already spent money on ads or launched to customers. BuyerLab AI helps identify those weaknesses before launch."

## 10-20 sec: Product Brief

"I will load the Wireless Earbuds Demo. It includes brand, model, product type, TRY price, local market, competitor context, proof assets, known limitations, and product-page copy."

## 20-35 sec: AI Buyer Simulation

"BuyerLab AI simulates four buyer personas: Skeptic Buyer, Bargain Hunter, Impulsive Buyer, and Trust Seeker. Each one returns a structured decision, purchase intent score, reason, objections, missing information, and suggested fix."

## 35-45 sec: Launch Readiness Report

"The Judge report turns the buyer reactions into a launch readiness verdict, simulated conversion score, buyer loss reasons, trust risk, price resistance, clarity risk, and required fixes before launch."

Important wording:

"This is not a real market prediction. It is an AI-simulated buyer reaction score to help sellers find product-page weaknesses."

## 45-55 sec: Market Context and Friction Map

"The dashboard shows heuristic local price perception, competitor gap, category expectation checks, and AI-simulated buyer attention by product page section. This is not real eye-tracking."

"For this earbuds demo, BuyerLab flags that the 799 TRY price needs stronger proof: battery life, warranty details, microphone proof, and a clear comparison against the lower-priced competitor."

## 55-60 sec: Fix Pack and Before/After

"Finally, BuyerLab AI creates a practical fix pack, applies the improved copy, and re-runs the simulation. The before/after view shows the simulated conversion score delta, improved sections, remaining risks, and next best actions."

## Closing Line

"BuyerLab AI gives sellers a faster pre-launch diagnostic loop: simulate buyers, find conversion blockers, improve the page, and test again before spending money on traffic."
